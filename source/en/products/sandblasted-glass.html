Sandblasted Glass

Sandblasted glass is produced by blasting silica sand or other abrasive materials at high pressure onto the surface of the glass. This erodes the surface, creating a rough, frosted look. Sandblasting allows for high precision and is ideal for creating intricate designs, detailed geometric patterns, corporate logos, or artistic murals on glass.

Key Features:

  • Design Flexibility: Capable of creating complex, custom patterns and gradients.
  • Privacy Control: Varying degrees of opacity can be achieved.
  • Durability: The design is physically etched into the glass.
  • Cost-Effective: economical method for custom decorative glass.
  • Lighting Effects: Can be edge-lit to make the etched design glow.

Applications:

  • Corporate logo branding on glass doors.
  • Decorative partitions in restaurants and hotels.
  • Privacy bands on office glazing.
  • Artistic glass panels.
  • Signage and wayfinding.
