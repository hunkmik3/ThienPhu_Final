Patterned Glass

Patterned glass, also known as textured or rolled glass, is made by passing molten glass between rollers, one of which carries a pattern. This imprints a distinct texture onto one or both sides of the glass. The texture diffuses light and obscures detailed vision, offering a balance between transparency, privacy, and decorative appeal.

Key Features:

  • Decorative: Adds visual interest and vintage or modern style to interiors.
  • Privacy: Distorts the view while still allowing light transmission.
  • Variety: Available in many classic and contemporary textures (reeded, fluted, rain, hammered).
  • Processable: Can be toughened/tempered for safety applications.
  • Light Diffusion: Softens harsh sunlight.

Applications:

  • Cabinet doors, kitchen cupboards.
  • Interior doors and partitions.
  • Shower screens.
  • Lighting fixtures.
  • Office dividers and meeting rooms.
