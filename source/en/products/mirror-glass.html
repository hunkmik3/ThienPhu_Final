Mirror Glass

Mirror glass is produced by coating a silver layer, a copper layer, and waterproof paint on the back of float glass. This process creates a highly reflective surface. Quality mirrors use high-grade float glass to ensure a distortion-free reflection. Mirrors are an essential element in interior design, used not just for grooming but to create the illusion of space and reflect light.

Key Features:

  • High Reflectivity: Clear, bright, and distortion-free images.
  • Durability: Multi-layer coating protects against corrosion and moisture (especially in bathrooms).
  • Space Enhancement: Makes small rooms appear larger and brighter.
  • Variety: Available in silver, bronze, grey, antique, and tinted finishes.
  • Safety Options: Can be backed with safety film to hold fragments if broken.

Applications:

  • Bathrooms and dressing rooms.
  • Wall cladding in gyms, dance studios, and lobbies.
  • Wardrobe doors.
  • Decorative furniture overlays.
  • Retail fitting rooms.
