Reflective Glass

Reflective glass is created by applying a metallic reflective coating during the float glass manufacturing process. This coating gives the glass a mirror-like appearance from the outside, reflecting a significant portion of incoming solar radiation. It provides excellent solar control and glare reduction while offering a one-way mirror effect for privacy during daylight hours.

Key Features:

  • Solar Control: Reflects heat, reducing cooling loads in buildings.
  • Glare Reduction: Improves visual comfort for occupants.
  • Daytime Privacy: Prevents outsiders from seeing in during the day.
  • Aesthetics: Available in various colors (bronze, grey, green, blue) for striking exterior designs.
  • UV Filtering: Reduces transmission of harmful UV rays.

Applications:

  • Skyscrapers and office building facades.
  • Curtain walls.
  • Windows exposed to intense direct sunlight.
  • Roof glazing and atriums.
