Company Overview

THIEN PHU TEAM

Under the leadership of Director Ho Van Hao, 162 Thien Phu employees are operating effectively at 2 factories.

The core strength of Thien Phu comes from this very collective: a dedicated, creative management team and a highly skilled technical workforce.

This synchronized combination creates a professional collective, always proactively mastering technology and strictly adhering to all production processes, ensuring the creation of quality and consistent products.

THIEN PHU CULTURE

Thien Phu culture is built on 4 core criteria

01

ABSOLUTE QUALITY

We are committed to strictly applying international standards. Product quality is honor and top responsibility.

02

CONSTANT SAFETY

"Safety" is a non-negotiable principle, the number one priority in every operating process.

03

OPERATIONAL SPECIALIZATION

We optimize every stage (from order receiving, production to transport) to ensure the entire operating process is smooth, precise, and rhythmic.

04

COMPETITIVE SPEED

The "Fast" principle is instilled in the working mindset, ensuring progress and meeting every customer requirement in a timely manner.

WHY CHOOSE THIEN PHU?

1. 20 YEARS EXPERIENCE (SINCE 2005)

Experience and deep market understanding are the foundation for us to bring the most optimal glass solutions.
