Production Complex

With modern machinery systems and advanced production processes, we ensure product quality meets international standards.

Automatic Glass Loading and Cutting Line

This is the critical initial stage, ensuring absolute precision for every Thien Phu product.

The process fully automates the loading of large glass sheets and cutting according to CNC programmed drawings. The line ensures absolutely precise cuts, optimizes materials, and minimizes errors, ready for subsequent processing stages.

Tempered Glass Production Line

Operating an automatic thermal tempering process, Thien Phu's line creates safety glass products with high bearing capacity, impact resistance, and thermal shock resistance.

This process ensures an absolute safe breakage mechanism (small granules) for users. With optimal transparency and light transmission, this is the ideal material for applications requiring luxury and durability.

Laminated Glass Production Line

Thien Phu's line produces laminated glass (safety laminated glass) — a superior safety and anti-intrusion solution for projects.

The process automates the bonding of two or more glass layers with special film layers (PVB/EVA), through heat and high pressure processing. The output product is resistant to penetration; even when broken, the glass fragments still adhere firmly to the film layer, ensuring absolute safety.

Insulated Glass Production Line

Thien Phu produces soundproof and heat-insulating glass (insulated glass) — the optimal solution for projects requiring high energy efficiency and quietness.

The process automates the assembly of glass layers, sealing an inert gas layer in the middle with specialized glue and aluminum spacers. The output product effectively prevents heat transmission and noise, bringing superior quietness and comfort.
